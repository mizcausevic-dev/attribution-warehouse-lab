model comparison

Different models tell different revenue stories on the same warehouse rows.

The point of attribution is not to find one magical truth. It is to make the tradeoffs obvious enough that RevOps, demand gen, and leadership can choose the right lens consciously.

First-Touch

Assigns full credit to the earliest attributable session in the warehouse journey.

  • Organic Search: $390,000 pipeline · ROI 500.0
  • Paid Search: $132,000 pipeline · ROI 235.71
  • Partner Referral: $94,000 pipeline · ROI 1175.0
Model name: first_touch
Last-Touch

Assigns full credit to the touchpoint immediately preceding the warehouse conversion event.

  • Sales Outreach: $390,000 pipeline · ROI 1392.86
  • Direct Traffic: $226,000 pipeline · ROI n/a
  • Organic Search: $0 pipeline · ROI 0.0
Model name: last_touch
Linear

Splits credit evenly across all attributable touches.

  • Organic Search: $130,000 pipeline · ROI 166.67
  • Sales Outreach: $130,000 pipeline · ROI 464.29
  • ABM Display: $70,000 pipeline · ROI 79.55
Model name: linear
Position Weighted

Assigns 40% to first touch, 40% to last touch, and spreads the remaining 20% across assists.

  • Organic Search: $156,000 pipeline · ROI 200.0
  • Sales Outreach: $156,000 pipeline · ROI 557.14
  • Direct Traffic: $90,400 pipeline · ROI n/a
Model name: position_weighted
